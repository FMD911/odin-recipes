Sesame Chicken

Description

Family recipe that was passed down for generations, perfect mix of rich taste and quick to prepare

Ingredients

  • 500gr Chicken Breast
  • 180gr Sour Cream
  • Sesame Seeds to preference
  • Vegeta or Salt to preference
  • Optional: Paprika

Steps

  1. Roast sesame seeds on a medium heat until golden brown
  2. Add oil and fry chicken until golden brown
  3. Add sour cream and mix until it starts "melting"
  4. Season per liking
  5. Enjoy!
