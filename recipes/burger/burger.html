Burger

Description

Juicy homemade burger with rich flavors, quick and easy to prepare

Ingredients

  • Burger bun
  • Beef patty
  • Cheese slice
  • Lettuce
  • Sauce of choice

Steps

  1. Grill the beef patty until cooked
  2. Toast the bun lightly
  3. Add cheese on hot patty
  4. Assemble burger with toppings
  5. Serve and enjoy!
