Pizza

Description

Classic homemade pizza with crispy crust and rich toppings

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Optional toppings

Steps

  1. Roll out pizza dough
  2. Spread tomato sauce
  3. Add cheese and toppings
  4. Bake until golden
  5. Slice and serve
